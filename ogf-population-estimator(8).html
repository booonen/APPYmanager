OGF population estimator

Fetch residential ways/relations, assign density presets, estimate population. Use density=N in JOSM to auto-assign.

Tag any residential way or multipolygon in JOSM with density=N (0-indexed). When fetched, the tool auto-assigns the matching preset. If no density tag is present, the element defaults to the first category and can be changed manually.

    Range = the preset's low/high band × area. Areas use the spherical excess formula (~0.1% match to JOSM). Multipolygon relations are supported: outer rings add area, inner rings subtract. Assumes the full polygon area is residential — exclude internal parks/commercial zones beforehand.

    Enter way and/or relation IDs (comma- or space-separated). A bare number is treated as a way.
